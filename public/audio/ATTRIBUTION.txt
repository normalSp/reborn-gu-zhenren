RebornG v0.7.1 Audio Attribution
=================================

Runtime source of truth:
- src/canon/audio-source-manifest.json
- public/audio/audio-source-manifest.json is a release mirror for inspection.

Policy
------
- Free public package: verified free BGM/SFX remain available as fallback.
- Nonprofit fan package: user-supplied community character themes are runtime-enabled only after local files are copied into public/audio.
- Root bgm/ is a staging folder only; runtime must reference public/audio paths.
- AI/FFmpeg/synthetic BGM placeholders are not allowed in runtime.
- Combat and achievement SFX use real free assets, not AI-generated placeholders.
